Guest Rooms

The Plaza King features one King bed and is located on floors 5–19. Bathroom is finished with inlaid earth stone mosaics and includes a luxurious bathtub and separate shower.

The Plaza’s Deluxe King has one King bed and provides 550 sq. ft. of living space. It includes a bathtub, separate shower, and luxury marble finishes.

The Connecting Deluxe features 1,025 sq. ft. with two connecting rooms: one King bed room and one two Queen bed room with luxury bathrooms.

The Grand Luxe King Room offers an open layout with seating area, King bed, butler pantry, and elegant marble bathroom with luxury fittings.
